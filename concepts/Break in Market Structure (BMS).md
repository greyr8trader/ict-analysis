Canonical Definition

A Break in Market Structure occurs when price violates a previously formed short-term swing high or swing low.

Identification Criteria

A swing high or low is formed.

Price subsequently trades through that level.

Explicit Rules (From Sources)

A short-term low being broken after a liquidity run constitutes a market structure break.
(Source: 2022 ICT Mentorship — Episode 2)

BMS is used after liquidity has been taken.

BMS confirms directional intent aligned with higher-timeframe bias.

Validity Conditions

Most effective after buy-side or sell-side liquidity has been attacked.
