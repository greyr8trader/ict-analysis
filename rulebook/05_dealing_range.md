# Dealing Range & Equilibrium

## Range Construction
- The current dealing range is defined by the session high and session low.

## Equilibrium
- The 50% level of the range represents equilibrium.
- Above the 50% level is premium.
- Below the 50% level is discount.

## Directional Use
- In bearish conditions:
  - Shorts SHOULD be taken in premium.
  - Targets SHOULD favor discount.
