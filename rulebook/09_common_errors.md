# Common Errors & Violations

## Dependency Errors
- Treating marked chart areas as trade signals.
- Expecting the model to identify trades in advance.
- Relying on external confirmation instead of independent analysis.

## Discipline Failures
- Ignoring assigned drills, study work, or review processes.
- Rushing development or attempting to bypass the learning curve.
- Adding complexity beyond the defined model.

## Psychological Errors
- Overtrading after profitable outcomes.
- Over-leveraging due to emotional response to gains or losses.
- Failing to accept responsibility for drawdowns or losses.

## Expectation Misalignment
- Expecting daily perfection or constant profitability.
- Abandoning the process due to frustration.
- Believing all individuals can succeed regardless of discipline or mindset.
