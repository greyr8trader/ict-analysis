## Market Structure Shift (MSS)
An intraday violation of a short-term swing high or low AFTER liquidity is taken,
indicating a likely intraday draw.

## Internal Range Liquidity (IRL)
Short-term highs/lows or imbalances within a price leg used for refined entries.

## Order Block
A change in the state of delivery, defined by the opening price of the candle
that begins a directional series.

## Fair Value Gap (FVG)
An imbalance created when price moves without candle overlap.

## Dealing Range
The session low-to-high used to define equilibrium, premium, and discount.

## Equilibrium
The 50% level of a defined dealing range.

## Displacement
An obvious, energetic movement in price that signals strong delivery.
Weak or lethargic movement is not sufficient.

## Swing High / Swing Low (3-Candle)
Swing High: middle candle higher high than candles on both sides.
Swing Low: middle candle lower low than candles on both sides.
